Site Plan: Healthy Catering and Delivery Services

Site Name

Proposed Name: Onyx Healthy Bites

Reason for Choosing: The name reflects the business focus on offering healthy meal delivery services specifically tailored for individuals in Abuja, Nigeria. "Healthy Bites" highlights nutritious meals in a catchy way, attracting a health-conscious audience.

Optional Domain Availability: onyxhealthybites.com

Site Purpose

The purpose of this site is to provide an accessible platform for customers in Abuja to explore healthy meal options and order deliveries. It will promote healthy living by offering meal plans, recipes, and fitness tips while allowing customers to manage their diet needs conveniently through the siteâ€™s online ordering system.

Scenarios

  • How can I order healthy meals and have them delivered to my door-step in Abuja?
  • What meal plans are available that focus on low-carb or high-protein diets?

Color Schema

Primary Color: Green (#28a745) - Used for headers, buttons, and accents to represent health and nature.

Secondary Color: White (#ffffff) - Used as the background color to maintain simplicity and cleanliness.

Typography

Font 1: Poppins (Google Font) - Used for headings to provide a modern, clean look.

Font 2: Open Sans - Used for body text to ensure readability and clarity for users.

Wireframe

Home Page (Mobile View):

  • Logo at the top center
  • Navigation menu (hamburger icon)
  • Introductory banner with a call-to-action button
  • Sections: Featured meal plans, Testimonials, Blog snippets
  • Footer with contact information and social media links

Home Page (Desktop View):

  • Logo and navigation bar at the top
  • Hero image with a call-to-action button
  • Three-column section: Meal Plans, Blog Highlights, Customer Reviews
  • Footer with quick links, contact info, and social media icons
