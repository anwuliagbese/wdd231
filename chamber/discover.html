Discover Agbese Chamber of Commerce

Discover Amazing Places

Location 1

Yankari Game Reserve, Bauchi State.

The Yankari Game Reserve is home to several natural warm water springs, as well as the remaining elephants and lions in Nigeria. Be sure to take a dive in the crystal clear warm springs, get a view of some wildlife, and your family vacation is set to be a memorable one.

Location 2

Idanre Hill, Akure, Ondo State

Idanre Hills is located in an ancient city set upon a hill in the city of Idanre Akure, Ondo State. Ensure you take the 682 steps it takes to get to the top of the hill and bask in the beautiful landscape of the city with your family.

Location 3

Obudu Mountain Resort, Cross River State

Looking for adventure and tranquillity!, Obudu Mountain Resort is for you!. This is one of Africa’s finest and one of the most beautiful places in Nigeria, located in the highlands and deep tropical forests of Cross River State. Enjoy the cable cars, canopy walkways and so much more with your family!.

Location 4

Olumo Rock, Abeokuta, Ogun State

Olumo rock is a historical monument that served as shelter for the locals during intercity wars. If the kids aren’t up for a hike up the rock, there are elevators available so they can still have a look at this beautiful place in Nigeria.

Location 5

Mpape Abuja, Nigeria

Mpape Crushed Rock is an abandoned quarry in Abuja. This beautiful place in Nigeria is such a picturesque location, perfect for a picnic with the family, a photoshoot, or a refreshing hike. Pack a basket, put on some comfortable shoes, and head over to this scenic wonder.

Location 6

Hi Impact Planet

Hi-Impact Planet offers world-class fun for the young and old, friends and families. There are games, rides and other entertaining attractions for the young and young at heart. You and your family will enjoy a good time and form magical memories.

Location 7

Central Mosque, Abuja

A great place to visit for local attractions.

Location 8

Abraka Gordons Beach Rsort, Delta State

This is a perfect spot for a quiet picnic away from the hustles and bustles of the city. Swimming, boat riding, fishing are some of the favorite activities here. Taking a walk on the sandy beach and watching the river flowing rhythmically and slowly is such a soul refreshing experience. Children’s playground, rackets/ badminton courts and lawn tennis court are some of the side attraction at this resort.
